Each industry has its own nuances in management styles, team culture, hiring practices, business development activities, etc. But its most important to look at the bigger picture to meet specific human resource needs.

That’s where we come in. The experts who provide specialized, value-added, custom solutions that exceed expectations. Rana is a Technology Contracting and Recruiting Firm with offices in California and India. We focus on providing employment solutions for IT, Technology, Infrastructure, Engineering and Next Generation Technologies.

We work with the biggest names in technology and services across industries and verticals. Our recruiters identify, screen and hire talent for entrylevel to executive-level positions in a range of sectors. As a consultative HR partner, Rana helps businesses spot and hire the right talent from outside while helping the business retain the best talent from the inside.

This is the reason we have the confidence of Tier 1 and Tier 2 software leaders, Start-Ups, Technology firms and are the most sought after firm for niche searches.

LEADERSHIP

Jim Holcomb
Chief Executive Officer

Jim is the CEO of Rana. He joined Rana after functioning as the Global Head of Contingent Staffing for Wipro Technologies for 8 years. Jim has 25 years of experience in the staffing and consulting industry beginning his career as a recruiter and with subsequent positions of branch manager, direct, vice president and president for staffing companies. He has also had roles on the buyer side for Bearing Point, Siemens, Microsoft and Wipro. Jim is responsible for all aspects of Rana operations and growth, driving execution for our clients and positioning us as a company founded on respect, honesty, dignity and acceptance. He is a noted industry speaker and writer and respected subject matter expert regarding contingent staffing and workforce management.
