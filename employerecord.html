SMART BENCH

SMART BENCH

Smart Bench Services from Rana is a proven, high volume solution for companies who do not wish to incur the cost, effort or risk in temporary or contingent payroll services. Our solutions contain a dedicated Program Management Office (PMO) led by contingent workforce industry veterans who serve as a single point of contact for each client program and end user. The day-to-day operations and end user engagement is supported by the program team and our corporate shared services, including HR, Legal, Payroll, Finance, and Accounting.

Payrolling solution

Our payrolling solution utilizes the PMO to provide full lifecycle management of each payrolled worker, the PMO is engaged from the initial end user request, to on-boarding and through off-boarding.

Rana has on-boarded thousands of workers throughout the U.S., Canada, and offshore. Our capabilities are not limited by industry.

Our clients select Rana because we understand the importance and value of the payroll population at an effective cost for our clients. Rana operates our service in such a manner that overall overhead is minimized and these cost savings are reflected in what we charge our clients. Many times, our clients can in turn pass these savings on to the payrolled individuals in terms of higher pay rates.
